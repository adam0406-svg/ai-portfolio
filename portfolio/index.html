About

The intersection
of AI and enterprise
reality.

Most AI portfolios prove you can call an API. Mine proves I understand what it takes for AI to land in an organisation — the technology, the change management, and the gap between them.

At Novo Nordisk, I took Extended Reality training from prototype to global rollout across a regulated pharmaceutical environment. I know first-hand that shipping technology is the easy part. Getting it adopted is the work.

Now I build AI solutions and study every dimension of what makes them fail or succeed in practice — technical, organisational, and strategic.
